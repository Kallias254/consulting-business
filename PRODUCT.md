# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Doctoral scholars (PhD candidates, masters students) stuck in their dissertation writing or seeking manuscript editing, who need expert, faculty-level advisory coaching and structural refinement.

## Product Purpose
ScholarCrafted provides elite, faculty-level academic coaching, methodology support, and editorial polish to help researchers move their projects from a persistent stall to final submission with complete integrity.

## Brand Personality
Empathetic, rigorous, authoritative. (A highly polished academic tone that instills confidence, reduces writer's block, and presents elite scholarship).

## Anti-references
SaaS-cream landing pages with generic layouts, low-contrast grey text, gradient text, identical grids of cards with icons, side-stripe borders, or generic modern AI-sounding copy.

## Design Principles
1. **Academic Dignity & Editorial Rigor**: The typography and layout should feel like a premium printed academic journal or high-end publisher, not a generic web application.
2. **Confidence Through Precision**: Crisp layout lines, zero-border-radius design, structured spacing, and precise copy build trust with senior faculty and candidates.
3. **Structured Focus**: Layouts are designed to break down overwhelming information into clear, rhythmic paths that avoid user anxiety.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA level compliance. Support for high-contrast text, clear visual keyboard focus indicators, and minimal, non-distracting kinetic movements for users sensitive to motion.
