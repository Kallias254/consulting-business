## 04. Critical Boundaries & Ethics

### Editing vs. Consulting
- **Editing (Asynchronous):** Reserved for manuscripts that have already been written. The goal is to polish, not to instruct.
- **Consulting (Live):** Real-time instructional support meant to help you learn how to plan, conduct, and report your research.

### Client Responsibilities
- **Draft Versioning:** You are responsible for ensuring the correct, final draft is submitted. Submitting a revised draft after editing has begun may incur additional fees.
- **Accuracy:** Editing does not include verifying the raw accuracy of your data or the validity of your references.

### Academic Integrity
We believe in absolute transparency. **Under no circumstances do our editors provide ghostwriting services.** We do not write your content, run your analysis, or conduct original research on your behalf.
