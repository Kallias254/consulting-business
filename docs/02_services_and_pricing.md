## Proposed Pricing Tiers

### The Foundation (The Gift of Stewardship)
*Offered as a success reward following the completion of a Tier 1 or Tier 2 project.*
- **Services:**
    - **Academic Dossier Ingestion:** We manually ingest your existing records (Education, Pubs, Talks) into our sovereign database.
    - **Prestige Render:** One-click generation of a typeset PDF CV via the Typst engine.
    - **The "Forward & Forget" Workflow:** Access to the concierge project inbox for easy record updates.
- **Pricing:** **FREE.**
- **Goal:** Celebrate a successful project submission by securing the client's career infrastructure, making the transition to the Tier 3 retainer frictionless.

### Tier 1: The Finisher (Editing & Formatting)
*For the researcher who is "done" but needs a professional polish.*
- **Services:**

- **Pricing Model:** Per Word / Per Page

- **Touch Level:** Low Touch, High Volume.

*For the researcher who needs expert guidance on methodology or data.*
- **Services:**

    - **Example Rate:** $150 - $300 per hour.
    - **Milestone Fee:** e.g., $2,500 for a full "Results" chapter write-up.
- **Touch Level:** Medium Touch, High Value.

### Tier 3: The Professional (Full-Cycle Publication Support)
*For the busy academic who wants to outsource the entire publication process.*
- **Services:**
    - Includes all services from **The Scholar** tier.
    - **Impact Portfolio:** A self-updating, high-end professional website synced to your dossier.
    - **Opportunity Engine:** Automated scanning for grants, fellowships, and publisher calls.
    - **Strategic Project Management:** We manage the project from initial draft to final submission.
    - **Simultaneous Publisher Submission:** For book proposals, we manage a multi-press strategy, submitting to multiple houses simultaneously to maximize leverage and reduce the risk of a single rejection (see "The Leverage Strategy" below).

- **Pricing Model:** Monthly Retainer or Project Success Fee
    - **Example Retainer:** $1,500 - $3,000 per month.
    - **Project Fee:** Flat fee ranging from $5,000 - $15,000 for end-to-end support.
- **Touch Level:** High Touch, High Profit.

## Strategic Advantage: The "Leverage" Strategy
Unlike automated services, we use Micah's insight to manage **Simultaneous Submissions**. 
- **The Logic:** Submitting a book proposal to 3-5 relevant houses at once (with disclosure) creates a "soft auction" environment. If one press expresses interest, we can notify the others to accelerate their review.
- **The Safety Net:** It protects the client (and our success fee) from the "all eggs in one basket" syndrome. If one press rejects, we are already in the pipeline elsewhere.

## Financial Guardrails: Protecting the Agency Margin

With our **75/25 payout model** (where researchers receive 75% of the net project fee), we must maintain strict pricing floors to ensure the agency remains profitable.

1.  **Minimum Project Fee:** $500. Anything lower makes the administrative cost of management higher than the profit.
2.  **The Net Split:** The 75/25 split is calculated *after* payment processing fees (e.g., Stripe/Wise, approx. 3%) and direct project expenses (e.g., specialized software licenses).
3.  **The Platform Fee:** 5% of the agency's 25% is allocated to "Infrastructure & Admin" to cover the cost of the Agency OS and technical management.
4.  **Value-Based Pricing:** We price based on the *result* (e.g., a published paper), not just the time. This allows us to maintain a high "Principal Profit" even with a high researcher payout.
